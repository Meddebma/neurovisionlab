    Mission

    At Neurovision Lab, we're at the forefront of applying artificial intelligence to neuroradiology. Our research focuses on developing cutting-edge AI solutions to enhance medical imaging analysis, improve diagnostic accuracy, and advance patient care in neurological conditions.

    ABOUT

    We are an interdisciplinary team of radiologists, computer scientists, and researchers working together to develop AI solutions for radiology and neurosciences. Our research focuses on two core areas: computer vision and natural language processing (NLP).

    In computer vision, we design deep learning models for tasks like segmentation, detection, and classification of pathologies across various medical imaging modalities. For NLP, we explore large language models to advance question answering, information extraction, text summarization, and workflow optimization in healthcare.

    We are dedicated to open science and reproducibility, making our datasets, code, and trained models publicly available whenever possible to encourage collaboration and accelerate advancements in the field.
